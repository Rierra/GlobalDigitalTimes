Editorial Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

Our Commitment to Quality

Global Digital Times is committed to delivering accurate, timely, and insightful technology news. We adhere to professional journalism standards in all our coverage.

Source Attribution

All articles cite their original sources. We link to primary sources whenever possible and clearly attribute information to its origin. Our coverage synthesizes reporting from established news organizations while adding context and analysis.

AI-Assisted Content

Some articles on Global Digital Times are created with the assistance of AI technology. All AI-assisted content is reviewed and edited for accuracy, clarity, and journalistic quality before publication. We use AI to help with research, drafting, and formatting, but human editorial judgment guides all publishing decisions.

Accuracy and Corrections

We strive for accuracy in all our reporting. If we make an error, we will correct it promptly and transparently. Significant corrections will be noted at the top or bottom of the affected article.

Independence

Our editorial decisions are independent of commercial considerations. We do not accept payment for coverage, and advertising does not influence our editorial content.

Timeliness

We prioritize breaking news and aim to publish important technology stories as quickly as possible while maintaining accuracy. Speed never compromises our commitment to getting the facts right.

Coverage Selection

We cover stories based on their newsworthiness, relevance to our readers, and broader implications for the technology industry. Our coverage areas include AI, robotics, cybersecurity, gaming, cloud computing, big tech, startups, and tech policy.

Contact and Feedback

We welcome reader feedback, corrections, and story tips. Please reach out through our contact channels if you have concerns about our coverage.
